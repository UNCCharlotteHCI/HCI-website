The Human-Computer Interaction Lab at UNC Charlotte is directed by Dr. Celine Latulipe, Dr. Heather Lipford, Dr. Mary Lou Maher, Dr. Khai Truong, and Dr. David Wilson. Our research covers a broad range of areas in HCI, including Creativity Support Tools, Novel Interactions, Social Networking, and Privacy/Security.
